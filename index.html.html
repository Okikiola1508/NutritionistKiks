// Professional Summary

Okikiola Adebimpe Adebo is a results-driven Social Media Video Editor and Content Strategist with over four years of hands-on experience producing high-performing video content for creator-led brands and product-based businesses.

Her signature strength lies at the intersection of creative storytelling and operational efficiency. She understands the psychology of scroll-stopping content — the rhythm of a hook, the weight of a transition, the precise moment a caption should appear — and translates that understanding into consistent, measurable audience growth.

From food and lifestyle brands to fabric retailers and personal wellness pages, Okiki has developed a platform-native editorial voice that adapts to any brand identity while maintaining the production quality that sets top-performing content apart.

She produces up to 15 polished videos per week, has lifted average engagement by 32%, and reduced editorial turnaround by 20% — all while scaling quality, not compromising it.
